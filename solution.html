Solution
Introducing Nemacolin’s all-in-one Trip Planner that makes discovering experiences and creating an itinerary a breeze
OVERVIEW

We created an interactive Trip Planner to save guests time and simplify their booking experience at Nemacolin. Using logic rooted in machine learning and qualitative research, the tool takes in prospective guest preferences to curate a unique combination of room, experience, and dining recommendations.

How We Know It Works

Currently, less than 50% of guests make reservations for activities and dining prior to arrival. A key measure of the Trip Planner’s success is whether it would increase users’ likelihood to make bookings before their stay. The final design was tested with 25 participants representative of Nemacolin’s target market in comparative usability tests and semi-structured interviews.

92%

of participants were highly likely to book reservations prior to arrival

42%

of participants strongly agree they would book earlier than usual
Impression of Trip Planner & Nemacolin Brand

We asked participants to describe their impression of Nemacolin before and after interacting with our design to see how our design influences the resort's brand image.

Key Features

The final design of the Trip Planner offers three key features that meet both Nemacolin and guests' needs.

Web-based, Mobile First

Allows guests to book on-the-go and brings five-star service into the digital guest experience

Data-Focused Design

Leverages key pieces of guest data to curate offerings, reducing paradox of choice and streamlining resort operations

Value Beyond Pre-Arrival

Empowers guest discovery of unique options and enables resort staff to continue to provide personalized experience on-site
