The Team

Thanks for stopping by! We're a team of five graduate students in Carnegie Mellon's Human-Computer Interaction Masters program. This project was completed as part of a 7-month Capstone Project and commissioned by our client Nemacolin Woodlands Resort. To get a better sense of our day-to-day during the project, take a look at our Medium page.

Gillis Bernard

Gillis is a product designer with a background in product management, qualitative and quantitative user research, and public policy. She is interested in using machine learning to personalize the user experience and foster meaningful human connections. She enjoys reading, running, and outdoors activities.

He is also interested in using machine learning to create proactive robotic systems. In his free time, he absolutely loves playing video games.
